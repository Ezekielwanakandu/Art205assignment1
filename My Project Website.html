TRENDING NEWS OF THE WEEK

1. Executive Summary: LORVA PLASTIC is a business venture aimed at establishing a thriving wholesale and retail plastic bags business in Liberia. The business will cater to the increasing demand for quality plastic bags across various industries, including retail, hospitality, food, and manufacturing. Our goal is to become the leading supplier of plastic bags in Liberia by offering a diverse range of high-quality, eco-friendly, and affordable plastic bag solutions.
2. Business Description: LORVA PLASTIC will operate as a wholesale and retail plastic bags supplier, providing a wide selection of plastic bags tailored to the needs of businesses in Liberia. Our product range will include shopping bags, garbage bags, food storage bags, industrial bags, and more. We will prioritize the use of environmentally friendly materials and production processes, aligning with the growing global concern for sustainability.
3. Market Analysis: Liberia presents a favorable market for plastic bags due to the growing retail sector, tourism, and various other industries. The demand for plastic bags is expected to rise, driven by the need for convenient packaging solutions. Moreover, the absence of strong local competition in this market segment creates a significant opportunity for LORVA PLASTIC to capture a substantial market share.
4. Target Audience: Our primary customer segments include: Retail businesses (supermarkets, convenience stores, boutiques) Restaurants, hotels, and hospitality establishments Manufacturing and industrial companies Event organizers and exhibitors
